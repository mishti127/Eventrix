Upcoming Events

Stay Updated and Participate Actively

The following events are scheduled for the current academic year. These events are planned to enhance technical skills, cultural awareness, and overall personality development of students.

Students are encouraged to actively participate in upcoming events to gain hands-on experience, improve teamwork, and explore their interests beyond academics.

Why Participate in Upcoming Events?

  • Gain practical knowledge and real-world experience
  • Improve communication and leadership skills
  • Showcase talents and technical abilities
  • Earn certificates and recognitions
  • Build strong academic and professional profiles

Registration and Participation Guidelines

  • Students must register before the event deadline
  • Participation is open to all departments
  • Team events require prior team formation
  • Certificates will be provided to participants
  • Winners will receive prizes and recognitions

Detailed information about each event, including rules and schedules, will be shared through official college notice boards and communication channels.

We encourage all students to make the most of these opportunities and actively participate in upcoming events to enrich their college experience.
