Home

Welcome to the official college events portal. This website is designed to provide complete and accurate information about various academic, cultural, and technical events organized by our institution. College events play an important role in shaping students' personalities by encouraging creativity, teamwork, leadership, and confidence.

Through this platform, students can stay updated about upcoming programs, event schedules, and activities conducted throughout the academic year. Participation in events helps students gain exposure beyond classrooms and enhances their overall learning experience.

About Fest

The Annual College Fest is a grand celebration conducted every year with great enthusiasm and student participation. It brings together students from different departments to celebrate culture, innovation, and talent. The fest provides opportunities for students to express themselves and showcase their skills in various domains.

The fest includes a variety of programs such as competitions, workshops, seminars, exhibitions, and cultural performances. These activities help students develop communication skills, problem-solving abilities, and leadership qualities, which are essential for their future careers.

Events

Our college organizes a wide range of events that are carefully planned to meet the interests of students from different academic backgrounds. Events are categorized mainly into cultural and technical events, ensuring balanced development of students.

Each event is conducted under the guidance of faculty members and student coordinators to ensure smooth execution and active participation.

Cultural Events

Cultural events promote creativity, self-expression, and appreciation of art and tradition. These events allow students to relax, enjoy, and showcase their artistic talents while building confidence and stage presence.

  • Solo and Group Dance Performances
  • Singing and Music Competitions
  • Drama, Skit, and Mime Shows
  • Fashion Show
  • Art and Photography Exhibitions

Technical Events

Technical events focus on enhancing students' technical knowledge and problem-solving skills. These events encourage innovation, logical thinking, and practical application of theoretical concepts learned in classrooms.

  • Coding Contests
  • Hackathons
  • Robotics Challenges
  • Technical Quiz
  • Paper Presentation and Project Expo
